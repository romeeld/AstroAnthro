Science Tourist

What?

A family science travel series

Who?

The Holbrook-Davé Family loves to travel and explore science & technology in exotic places like Cape Town and in our own backyard like Washington D.C.! Romeel Davé explores history of science, physics, mechanics, and scientific breakthroughs. Jarita Holbrook explores astronomy, women in science, diversity, and enjoys interviewing scientists excited about their research. Mirabai explores water, aquaculture, plants, and cool places like aquariums. Jasmina, the youngest explores restaurants, local culture, local & ancient textiles, and she cooks for the family.

Where?

You can see our explorations of Edinburgh, United Kingdom, Washington D.C., USA, and Cape Town, South Africa!
